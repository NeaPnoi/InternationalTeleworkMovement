About the Movement

The International Telework Movement is a global initiative dedicated to advancing the widespread adoption of remote work. We are focused on helping organizations successfully transition to telework by providing resources, guidance, and support for creating effective remote-first environments. Our mission is to empower businesses and individuals to thrive in a world where flexible, distributed work becomes the norm.
We also advocate for teleworkers by connecting them with suitable work environments, ensuring they have access to the tools and support they need to succeed remotely. Beyond individual and organizational support, the movement aims to normalize telework as a standard practice across industries, breaking down barriers to remote work adoption.
When necessary, the International Telework Movement applies political pressure to push for policies that protect and enhance the rights of teleworkers, ensuring fair access to remote work opportunities and legal protections for all. Through collaboration with companies, governments, and other stakeholders, we are leading the charge toward a more flexible, inclusive, and future-proof work culture.
